Rules & regulations

Please read and agree to the burial rules for Daarul Barzakh Maqbarah before signing.

I The Daarul Barzakh Maqbarah is the sole property of Al Maad Al Islami Inc. All rules must be agreed upon and strictly observed by all visitors.
II No plots will be deeded to anyone before or after burial. The entire cemetery property will continue to remain under the ownership of Al Maad Al Islami Inc.
III Upon application and payment of dues, Al Maad Al Islami Inc. will allocate a cemetery lot and authorize the burial. Al Maad Al Islami reserves the right to deny authorization without reason. Reservations for specific lot locations or family lots are not allowed.
IV All burials shall take place under the supervision of an authorized Al Maad Al Islami representative. The washed and prepared body must be received at the cemetery no later than one hour before sunset.
V No caskets shall be used for burial. The body will be buried in a shroud and a pressure-treated plywood or alternative wooden material to create a small cavity before backfilling the grave.
VI The top of all graves shall initially be raised no more than 1 inch and will be grassed once the soil settles and the top becomes flat.
VII Graves will be marked with a standard stone marker, flushed with the ground, engraved with the name and date of birth and death of the deceased. Al Maad Al Islami Inc. will provide and install this marker.
VIII Unauthorized markers, monuments, permanent or temporary decorations, flowers, plants, and shrubs are not permitted. Any violation will result in removal at the family's cost.
IX Picture taking and/or videotaping of any kind during burial is strictly prohibited. Al Maad Al Islami reserves the right to refuse any ritual before, during, or after burial if it has no grounds in Islamic jurisprudence.
X Children under 15 years must be accompanied by adults. No pets are allowed. Parking is allowed only in designated areas.
XI The cemetery will remain gated and locked except during published times when it is open for family/visitors. Out-of-town visitors may contact Al Maad Al Islami Inc. for permission to visit at other times.
XII Al Maad Al Islami Inc. shall not be responsible for any damage or loss to property or person while on cemetery premises and reserves the right to refuse entry to any person.
